AKKA UNIVERSITY

Akka University is a public state university located in Tamil Nadu, India.
The main campus is in Chennai.
It was originally established on 4 September 1978 and was named after C.N.
The University is now the affiliating authority for close to 550 engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu.
